Delete Account & Data

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data from the Sant Narhari Sonar app.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account using either of the following methods:

Option 1: Delete from Within the App

Open the Sant Narhari Sonar app and navigate to:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm deletion. Your request will be processed automatically.

Option 2: Request via Email

Send an email to:

santnarhari.sonar@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The phone number or email address associated with your account
  • Your registered name (for verification)

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials and authentication data
  • Profile information (name, photo, contact details)
  • Family directory registration
  • Matrimony profile (if created)
  • Business listings and advertisements
  • Event RSVPs and participation records
  • Posts, comments, and uploaded content
  • Subscription and payment history
  • App preferences and settings

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period for legal, security, or operational reasons:

  • Transaction records: Payment transaction IDs may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Backup systems: Data in encrypted backups may persist for up to 30 days before automatic deletion
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for analytics

Deletion Timeframe

Your account and personal data will be deleted within 7 days of receiving your request.

You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You will not be able to recover your data.
  • If you have an active subscription, please cancel it before requesting deletion to avoid future charges.
  • You may create a new account after deletion, but previous data will not be restored.

Contact Us

If you have questions about data deletion or privacy, contact us at:
