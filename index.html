Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Sant Narhari Sonar ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Sant Narhari Sonar mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Phone number, email address, name, and profile photo when you register.
  • Profile Data: Family details, occupation, address, and other information you choose to add to your profile.
  • Matrimony Information: If you create a matrimony profile, we collect details such as age, education, occupation, family background, and preferences.
  • Business Information: Business name, category, contact details, and description if you register a business.
  • Payment Information: We use Razorpay for payment processing. We do not store your complete payment card details.
  • User Content: Photos, posts, event RSVPs, and other content you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide community directory and matrimony services
  • Send notifications about events, news, and community updates
  • Process subscription payments
  • Enable communication between community members
  • Improve and personalize the App experience
  • Ensure compliance with community guidelines

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Community Members: Your profile information (as per your privacy settings) is visible to other registered members of the Sonar community.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, and Razorpay for payments.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication using Firebase Auth
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data in the App
  • Update: Modify your profile information anytime
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications through App settings

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and storage - Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay: Payment processing - Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
